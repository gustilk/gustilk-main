Refund Policy
Last updated: March 2026

This policy explains how refunds work for Gûstîlk subscriptions and purchases.

1. Subscription Cancellation
You can cancel your subscription at any time through:
- Your Gûstîlk account settings
- Your Apple App Store subscription settings (iOS users)
- Your Google Play subscription settings (Android users)

When you cancel, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

2. Refund Eligibility
We offer refunds in the following situations:
- You were charged after cancelling your subscription
- You were charged twice for the same subscription period
- A technical error prevented you from accessing premium features you paid for
- You cancelled within 48 hours of your first subscription purchase and have not used any premium features

We do not offer refunds for:
- Partial subscription periods
- Subscriptions that have already been used
- Change of mind after the 48-hour window
- Accounts terminated due to violations of our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines

3. App Store Purchases
If you subscribed through the Apple App Store or Google Play, refund requests must be submitted directly to Apple or Google:
- Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
- Google Play: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions

Apple and Google handle all billing for in-app purchases and their refund policies apply.

4. How to Request a Refund
To request a refund directly from Gûstîlk, contact us at:
support@gustilk.com

Please include:
- Your registered email address or phone number
- The date of the charge
- The reason for your refund request

We will review your request and respond within 5 business days.

5. Processing Time
Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days. The time for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider.

6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at gustilk.com/refund.

7. Contact Us
For refund questions or requests:
support@gustilk.com
